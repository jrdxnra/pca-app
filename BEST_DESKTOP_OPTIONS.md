## Form Factor Options

### Small Form Factor (SFF)
- **Size**: ~15" x 7" x 7"
- **Pros**: Compact, fits anywhere
- **Cons**: Less upgradeable, limited expansion
- **Best for**: Small spaces, minimal setup

### Mini PC / Tiny
- **Size**: ~7" x 7" x 2"
- **Pros**: Very small, low power
- **Cons**: Limited upgradeability
- **Best for**: Minimal space, always-on server

### Mid Tower (Standard)
- **Size**: ~18" x 8" x 17"
- **Pros**: Most upgradeable, best cooling
- **Cons**: Takes more space
- **Best for**: Maximum flexibility, custom builds
