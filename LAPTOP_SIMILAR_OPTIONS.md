## What to Check Before Buying

- ✅ **RAM is upgradeable** (some have soldered RAM)
- ✅ **Storage is NVMe SSD** (not SATA)
- ✅ **Warranty included** (at least 1 year)
- ✅ **Return policy** (30 days is good)
- ✅ **Reviews** (check for common issues)
