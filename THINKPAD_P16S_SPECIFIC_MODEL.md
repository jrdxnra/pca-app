# ThinkPad P16s - Specific Model Information

## Model Year/Generation

**Look for: ThinkPad P16s Gen 1 (2023/2024)**

This is the current generation with the specs I mentioned:
- AMD Ryzen 7 PRO 7840U (8 cores, 16 threads)
- Up to 32GB DDR5 RAM
- 1TB NVMe SSD option
- 16" display

## Model Numbers to Look For

### AMD Version (Recommended):
- **21J9** - ThinkPad P16s Gen 1 AMD
- **21JA** - ThinkPad P16s Gen 1 AMD (alternative model number)

### Intel Version (Also Available):
- **21J8** - ThinkPad P16s Gen 1 Intel
- Uses Intel i7-1355U or i7-1365U

## How to Identify the Right Model

When searching, look for:
1. **"ThinkPad P16s Gen 1"** or **"P16s (1st Gen)"**
2. **Model number starting with 21J9 or 21JA** (AMD version)
3. **AMD Ryzen 7 PRO 7840U** in the specs
4. **Released in 2023/2024**

## Where to Find Model Info

### On Lenovo.com:
- Product page will say "ThinkPad P16s Gen 1"
- Model number in product details (21J9, 21JA, etc.)
- CPU listed as "AMD Ryzen 7 PRO 7840U"

### In Product Name:
- Full name: "ThinkPad P16s Gen 1 (21J9) AMD"
- Or: "ThinkPad P16s (1st Gen) AMD"

## What to Avoid

- ❌ **ThinkPad P16** (without "s") - Different model, heavier
- ❌ **ThinkPad P15s** - Older model, different generation
- ❌ **ThinkPad P14s** - Smaller 14" version
- ❌ Older generations without "Gen 1" designation

## Exact Specs to Look For

**CPU Options:**
- ✅ AMD Ryzen 7 PRO 7840U (8 cores, 16 threads) - **Best**
- ✅ AMD Ryzen 5 PRO 7540U (6 cores, 12 threads) - Good but not as powerful
- ✅ Intel i7-1355U (10 cores, 12 threads) - Good alternative
- ✅ Intel i7-1365U (10 cores, 12 threads) - Good alternative

**RAM Options:**
- ✅ 32GB DDR5 - **What you want**
- ⚠️ 16GB DDR5 - Works but not "destroy everything" tier
- ❌ 8GB - Not enough

**Storage Options:**
- ✅ 1TB NVMe SSD - **Recommended**
- ✅ 512GB NVMe SSD - Minimum acceptable
- ❌ 256GB - Too small
